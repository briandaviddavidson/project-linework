=============================================
Angular: A linework set from Project Linework
=============================================

Traced and expanded by Dennis McClendon.

Derived from a map found in the 1980 Annual Report of W. R. Grace & Company, which Dennis McClendon “happened upon circa 1983,” and which he maintained in his inspiration file for many years. The Canadian provinces are his own addition.

Released to the public domain. Please credit the author and the project wherever possible.
